Introducing DHRIFT

Digital Humanities Resource Infrastructure for Teaching Technology (DHRIFT) is an open educational resource (OER) and publication platform for teaching and learning digital skills. Designed by humanities scholars for humanities scholars, DHRIFT includes a core set of peer reviewed and tested curricula covering common digital humanities (DH) topics. With DHRIFT you can create interactive, minimal computing, and accessibility-aware websites to support workshops, institutes, and courses. DHRIFT's core curriculum benefits from extensive classroom testing and has been used as the basis for workshops and classes at more than 20 colleges and universities across the United States.
